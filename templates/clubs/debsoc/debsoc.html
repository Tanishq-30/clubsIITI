DEbating Club

Description

One of the most respected clubs in IIT Indore, the Debating Society has a long standing tradition of excellence. With various events like Extempore, Parliamentary Debates, and MUNs, this club presents a plethora of opportunities for students. On the way, they also get to polish their communication skills, and gain self-confidence. The events are also known for their fun filled atmosphere. Though it may sound hard to believe, all you need to be a part of this club is a sincere interest.

Events

The Debating Society brought to you Alter Ego: The Turncoat Competition, a unique format of debating in which one literally debates oneself. Annual Word Wars Extempore- An impromptu speech which the candidate is required to make on a topic given there and then. No prior preparation is permissible. Roasts and Burns- Another impromptu event which requires the participant to roast celebrities and other participants to the best of their abilities. the Debating Society organized Elocution Competition as part of the Ek Bharat Sresht Bharat(EBSB) initiative.
