Electronics CLUB

EVENTS TEAMS ACHIEVEMENTS

Electronics Club is a student-run organisation at IIT Indore. We are firm believers that the most innovative and creative solutions arise from the diversity of thought. Therefore, we form interdisciplinary teams of undergraduates from all corners of campus to connect, collaborate, and create. By providing a resource-rich organisation for IIT Indore students interested in designing, building, and testing Electronics for a multitude of purposes, we hope to incite collaboration across multiple engineering disciplines. Our vision is to turn the theory students learn from their classes into real products that teams can take ownership of.
