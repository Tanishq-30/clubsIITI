Developer Students Club

EVENTS TEAMS ACHIEVEMENTS

Developer Student Clubs are university-based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. IIT Indore is part of the few institutes that have been selected for this program. For more information refer to https://developers.google.com/programs/dsc/ DSC IITI will be forming its Core Team, responsible for managing/organizing and even hosting the events. We are looking for students passionate about technology with a zeal to learn and work hard. And to reiterate, apart from improving your management and communication skills through a Google-sponsored program, you get: Direct contact with Google Experts and other DSC communities around the country. Collaborations on many innovative projects. Google goodies and swag ranging from bottles to t-shirts. Official Certificates. Invites to Google Developers Events. DSC IITI Core Team will have 2 divisions: the Technical division and the Non-Technical division. We expect students applying for the technical team to have passion as well as relevant experience in the fields of Android, Web Technologies, ML or Cloud so that they can regularly collaborate for interactive workshops on the same. Similarly, the non-technical team to have relevant experience in management and/or operations with a general inclination towards technology.
