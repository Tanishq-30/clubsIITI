Concreate Club

Description

We are a diverse group of students who loves to build and create stuff. The club is a student body which finds its place under the science and Tech Council, Student Gymkhana, IIT Indore. We are a group of super enthusiastic people who likes to implement the theoretical knowledge into building cool stuff. The Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) Club is a independent and premium student organisation certified by Mechanical engineering department, IIT Indore. The aim of the firm is to deal with common engineering problems and try to develop its optimum solution using CAE softwares. Our non- profit association helps young undergraduates to learn relevant designing skills,aware them about the use of CAE softwares in real industries and motivate them to participate in different design competitions. The student body also conducts some workshop (software-based and hands-on), seminars, webinars and different CAD competitions. We believe what knowledge we inherited we pass in on to young one

Events

AutoCAD workshop  AutoCAD 2D and 3D designing software used for designing floor plans of buildings etc. Staad Pro workshop  Staad Pro – Load analysis software used to analyse behaviour of structures under load.  Autodesk Revit - building information modelling software. Structura  The technical event of the concreate club in the techno-cultural fest FLUXUS was STRUCTURA.  In this event the participants were asked to build earthquake resistance building out of popsicle sticks and bamboo sticks.  Minimum 3 floor building was necessary and additional floors were given extra marks.  These structures were tested by a machine which shakes the base of the building and then breakage oof the building was observed. Bridge designing competition  Participants were asked to make truss bridge out of popsicle sticks.  Truss analysis workshop was also conducted prior to the competition.  Load testing was done by hanging weights on the deck of the bridge.  Winners were awarded by medals and cash prizes.
