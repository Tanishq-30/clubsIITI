Dramatics Club

Description

The Dramatics Club is a student organisation of IIT Indore dedicated to dramatics. The club has been, since it's inception, involved in performing both inside and outside the campus. Dramatics club IIT Indore is an open club for IITI students which organizes dramas on special occasions and national festivals.
