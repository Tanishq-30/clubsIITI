ASTRONOMY CLUB

EVENTS TEAMS ACHIEVEMENTS

We are a group of engineers passionate about everything above the Karman Line, all the way to the furthest reaches of the Cosmos! We conduct regular events - including workshops, quizzes and hackathons - both within and outside the IIT Indore community. We are constantly working on exciting and innovative projects to create new knowledge and hone skills in space science and technology!
