Description

Fine Arts Club, fondly called as 'FAC', is a group dedicated to promoting all aspects of fine arts, including graphic design and visual art. FAC conducts activities all through the year to stimulate and encourage artistic endeavours and bring out the artist in each of us . The activities serve as an excellent platform for learning, whereby even absolute beginners find themselves picking up brushes and making confident strokes in no time. The Club members, especially the newer members, are also given ample opportunities to participate in art competitions. At FAC, it is believed that art is not limited to just paints and brushes but can be produced out of anything! This has resulted in an atmosphere that promotes creativity, experimentation and collaboration in order to create something new.
