Kalakriti CLUB

EVENTS TEAMS ACHIEVEMENTS

Kalakriti Club is a group dedicated to promoting all aspects of arts.It conducts activities all through the year to stimulate and encourage artistic endeavours and bring out the artist in each of us . The activities serve as an excellent platform for learning, whereby even absolute beginners find themselves picking up brushes and making confident strokes in no time. The Club members, especially the newer members, are also given ample opportunities to participate in art competitions. At Kalakriti Club, it is believed that art is not limited to just paints and brushes but can be produced out of anything! This has resulted in an atmosphere that promotes creativity, experimentation and collaboration in order to create something new.
